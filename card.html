Goat

Goats have an incredible ability to climb. Their agility and balance allow them to navigate steep and rocky terrains with ease.

  • Scientific Name: Capra aegagrus hircus
  • Average Length: 1.5 meters
  • Average Lifespan: 16 years
  • Habitat: mountains, grasslands, and agricultural areas

Goats are domesticated animals known for their agility and adaptability. Part of the genus Capra, they are raised worldwide for their milk, meat, and wool. Characterized by their curved horns and beard, goats are intelligent, social creatures that can thrive in diverse environments thanks to their efficient digestive systems.
